Life

Abdul Kalam was born on October-15 1931 Rameswaram. In 1955 Abdul moved to Chennai(Madras then). He studied in Madras University. Where he had an incident with the dean; The dean was distatisfied with the lack of progress Abdul was putting. He said "If you don't finish your work your scholarship will be taken" He worked night and day and finally finished his work. the dean said since you coudn't be a fighter pilot(Which was him dream), Since he was a ninth qualifier and there were only 8 seats. In 1960 Abdul graduated from the Madras university and work in the DRDO(The defence research development Organization), Put by the government of India. He was also working in the NICOSPAR with Vikram Sarabhai 1963-1964 was when he went to vist NASA's Langley Research Center in Hampton, Virginia, USA. 1969 was when he was transfered to ISRO to work on the Satalite launch Vehicle or SLV which was a sucess. It was called the Rohini satelite. Abdul in the 1970's and 90's tried to make and deploy the Polar Satelite Lanch vehicle. Which both were sucessful 1981 and 1990 was when Abdul was awarded the Padma Bushan and Padma Vibushan respectively. 7 years after reciving the Padma Bushan and Padma Vibushan. Abdul revived the Highest Civilian award- the Bharat Ratna. 2002 was the year was when he became the president of India. Winning 922,884, surpassing the 107,366 votes won by Lakshmi Sahgal. 2007- after 5 years of president Abdul leaves the Rashtripati Bavan. Kalam visted Tamilnadu imposed Bihar's President's rule. In 2015 In Shilong. He breathed his last. His body was airlifted to Delhi. It went to Kerla where it was cremated.
